Why XMPP then?

On the XMPP side your messages will always keep in secret if you want so, you can even host your own xmpp server if you want but there are many public servers that you can use it for free!

XMPP support different end-to-end encryption methods such as OMEMO, OpenPGP and Legacy OpenPGP (not recommended).

That means even the server can read what you are sending the encryption and decryption keys are stored on you machine locally.

XMPP is also lightweight, there is no unnecessary shit like the spyware mentioned before.

XMPP support multiple clients.
